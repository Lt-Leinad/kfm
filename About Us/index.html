About Us

Kevin Feddy Media Limited

Kevin Feddy

Director

Kevin Feddy is the Director of Kevin Feddy Media. He began his career in 1986 as a general news reporter. Then he became the reporter for the Bury, Bolton and Wigan district office before landing the role of Manchester Airport reporter in 1996, covering the airport and surrounding districts

He has a deep understanding of business, the media and its workings and a keen eye for a story led him to secure the role as Business Editor for the Manchester Evening News in 2009.

In 2013 he established Kevin Feddy Media offering PR, media training and copywriting services to companies and entrepreneurs across a range of sectors.

His vast-experience enables his clients to obtain outstanding editorial coverage in print, online, on TV and radio, raising their profile and helping to drive their business performance.
