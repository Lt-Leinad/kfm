Vast Experience

Kevin Feddy is a vastly-experienced and respected journalist who worked on daily newspapers for more than 25 years. He spent most of his career at leading regional title the Manchester Evening News, where he was business editor until 2013, when he established his own media relations, copywriting and media training agency. In total, he worked for more than 12 years on the MEN’s business desk, building a fantastic network of contacts.

High Quality Services

Kevin Feddy Media provides high-quality media relations to a broad client base, from professional services practices, ambitious entrepreneurs, fast-growing small and medium-sized companies to large corporates and organisations. Services include planning and implementing strategic PR campaigns, writing and distributing news releases, opinion pieces, copy for client websites and for social media.

Great Customer Service

An integral part of our service is understanding clients’ needs to ensure their key messages are conveyed and their key goals are met through our output to carefully-targeted audiences.
