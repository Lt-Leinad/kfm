Vast Experience

Kevin Feddy is a vastly-experienced and respected journalist who worked on daily newspapers for more than 25 years. He spent most of his career at leading regional title the Manchester Evening News, where he was business editor until 2013, when he established his own media relations, copywriting and media training agency. In total, he worked for more than 12 years on the MEN’s business desk, building a fantastic network of contacts.

High Quality Services
